UNDERSTANDING TRADE SHOW SERVICES

Do you have a trade show coming up and confused on where to start? Let’s say say you have purchased or already own a trade show booth of your dreams that will separate you from your competitors, help you stand out in a crowded marketplace and eventually aid with helping increasing leads and overall revenue. Experienced exhibitors know that the right display is only a half the battle of a successful show. In order for you show to run smoothly you need to order trade show services such as labor for setup and dismantle your booth, logistics for shipping the display to and from the expo center, audio visual if your display requires, electricians for power running to your exhibit and rigors if you have anything that needs to be hung such as hanging signs or specialized lighting.

Undoubtedly, all these tasks can be very daunting. But don’t worry, we won’t leave you stranded with all these problems. Here at Exhibe, we can help you to find a solution for every situation you may encounter at your next trade show event. Our knowledgeable and highly trained staff can manage everything so you can focus on what’s important: getting more clients. Hence our service is at the heart of our business.  We pride ourselves on being able to deliver efficiently and on time, every time.

What Trade Show Services We Provide

When you have your trade show event on the horizon, the last thing you need to worry about is the tedious task or filling out paperwork to order your trade show services. With the show approaching every minute counts, and shipping your display and other materials can turn into a trouble, especially for newbies exhibiting for the first time. You can fortunately hand over all the logistics operations to us. We can arrange shipping of your trade show display materials to and from the show. If your exhibit was designed and custom crated we will ensure everything is packed properly. Such an attention to details not only prevents damage but also helps reduce drayage costs. We know that a dollar saved in is like a dollar earned. With our expertise we are sure to save you some money.

Having qualified show labor to set up and dismantle your booth can save loads of time, stress and money. We can take care of ordering the show labor and provide onsite show supervision to ensure everything runs smoothly as possible. When the show is over we will take care of packing and shipping items to your destination or our warehouse for storage.
