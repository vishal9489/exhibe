EXHIBE’S MODULAR DISPLAY STANDS

Looking to get into trade show marketing, but concerned about the adaptability of traditional types of exhibits? Say goodbye to those negative thoughts and say hello to the wonders of modular display stands that Exhibe Corporation is proud to present. These modern sleek trade show exhibits have a feel of custom made stands. This guarantees that they will make you noticed by potential customers and business partners. A professionally looking modular booth will help your company stand out from the crowd, and give you a prime opportunity to attract the business you deserve.

Our modular displays are of great value for exhibitors not only due to their superior look. These exhibits will open a whole new world of flexibility in terms of what you can do with your exhibit. They can be used in different arrangements, which means you can customize them to fit your trade show display needs and exhibit in a diverse array of locations. These universal and re-configurable exhibits provide your business with a sense of professionalism and will grow with your company for years to come.

Modular Display Kits and Custom Options

In order to have a display that people will notice, it has to be of great quality, function properly, have an appealing design, and be properly staffed for maximum traffic flow. With our amazing product selection you will surely to run down a modular displays kit or custom trade show exhibit options that fits your needs or suits your tastes.

Depending on your demands, you can select among a wide range of our high-tech and modern modular display stands. By complementing your booth with trade show graphics and accessories you can create a genuine custom looking exhibit. When selecting a modular display you need to take into consideration your budget and the core focus of your company. Thus, your booth has to convey your brand and be able to communicate your message amongst show visitors.

Having a modular exhibit is an effective way to showcase your products and services, and due to the versatility it can be a cost-effective marketing tool that is used at multiple shows.

With modular exhibits, the size of a booth can be reconfigured and adjusted to something that is more suitable for your trade show booth space. This is a good idea for those that want to keep the expenses of exhibiting down without having to compromise on quality or value.

Benefits of Our Modular Trade Show Booth Products

  • Your modular display stands can be re-configurable for different display area sizes and layouts. You can re-arrange, combine and substract booth’s components depending on your trade show needs getting the flexibility you desire.
  • Made from lightweight aluminium extrusion, and fitted with roll-up or stretch fabric graphics. This makes our modular displays highly durable and great to look at.
  • Available in kit form, or a customized option is possible depending on your wants, needs, and budget. However you plan to use a modular display, we can make it work for you.
  • Our modular trade show displays are ideal for frequent travel and heavy use. If you exhibit in a variety of locations, then a modular display could be the answer to your prayers!
